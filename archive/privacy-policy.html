Privacy Policy for OptiScan

Last updated: April 16, 2026

OptiScan ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information OptiScan accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Access

  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA): used to scan barcodes and QR codes.
  • Photos/Media (if granted): used only when you choose to save generated barcode images to your device.

How Information Is Used

Scanning and barcode generation are performed for app functionality. By default, scanned content and generated barcodes are handled on-device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your scanned content with third parties except when required by law.

Data Retention

Data you save (for example, generated barcode images) remains on your device unless you delete it.

Children's Privacy

OptiScan is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can deny camera or media permissions in your device settings. Some app features may not work without those permissions.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: islandtimestudiohq@gmail.com
